About me

My name is Hugo Laviec, I am a third-year Ph.D. student at the University of Brest (France). I am currently working on the development of new optical imaging modalities and their applications to bioimaging in collaboration with biologists.

Since my first biophotonics course during my Master's, my interest in innovative imaging techniques has only grown. The rich history of microscopy, both theoretical and experimental, is my favorite field of physics. I enjoy exploring new (and old) techniques that reveal unique contrasts in biological samples.
